Help and Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and easy:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the page.
  2. Fill out the required information in the registration form.
  3. Click on the "Submit" button to create your account.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit our website and browse the menu.
  2. Select the items you want to order by adding them to your cart.
  3. Review your order in the cart and proceed to checkout.
  4. Provide your delivery address and payment details.
  5. Confirm your order and wait for delivery!

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  2. Enter your email address associated with your account.
  3. Check your email for instructions on resetting your password.
